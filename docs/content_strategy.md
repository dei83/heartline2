# Blog Content Strategy & Pillars

## Core Philosophy
**Content → Dwell Time → Ad Impressions → Revenue**
Drive SEO traffic, engage users with high-quality insights, and funnel them to the Message Library and Premium features.

## The 5 Content Pillars

### 1. Psychology of Communication
**Goal:** Build trust by explaining *why* messages work.
- **Topics:** "Why 'Happy Birthday' Feels Empty", "The 3-Second Rule", "The Science of Gratitude".
- **Structure:** Hook -> Research/Expert Quote -> Real Examples (Good vs Bad) -> Key Takeaways -> CTA.

### 2. Real Stories & Case Studies
**Goal:** Emotional connection & high dwell time.
- **Topics:** "I Said The Wrong Thing at a Funeral", "The Birthday Message That Saved a Friendship".
- **Structure:** Situation -> What Went Wrong -> The Better Approach -> Psychological Context -> Reader Engagement.

### 3. Reddit Research Insights
**Goal:** Unique, data-backed content + SEO authority.
- **Topics:** "I Analyzed 10,000 Reddit Comments...", "What r/relationships Taught Me About Apologies".
- **Structure:** Methodology -> 5 Surprising Findings -> Practical Application -> 10 Verified Messages.

### 4. Relationship Dynamics
**Goal:** Search traffic for specific relationship problems.
- **Topics:** "Thanking Your Boss Without Sucking Up", "Navigating Parent Relationships", "Setting Boundaries".
- **Structure:** Problem Definition -> Power Dynamics/Psychology -> Effective Methods -> Pitfalls -> Cultural Considerations.

### 5. Cultural & Etiquette Guides
**Goal:** Niche SEO + International traffic.
- **Topics:** "Holiday Greetings Guide", "Inclusive Language", "Regional Differences".
- **Structure:** Comparison (e.g., Merry Christmas vs Happy Holidays) -> Holiday Guides -> Inclusive Language -> Real Examples.

## Workflow

1.  **Research (1-2h):** Reddit analysis, keyword research, finding the "emotional hook".
2.  **Outline (30m):** Hook, Key Takeaways, Section breakdown, Psychological Insight, Message Links.
3.  **Drafting (2-3h):** Conversational tone, short paragraphs, personal anecdotes.
4.  **Optimization (30m):** SEO Title/Meta, Internal Links, Ad Placements.
5.  **Publish (15m):** Social scheduling, email newsletter.
